SPAGHETTI SAUCE

is a recipe for a SPAGHETTI SAUCE. The sauce is made with cooked Spaghetti, Olive oil, Garlic gloves, minced, Red pepper flakes, crushed Tomatoes, salt and pepper, fresh basil leaves. It's a healthy and flavorful sauce that's perfect for lunch or as a side dish for dinner. You can customize the sauce by adding your favorite veggies, nuts, or seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450 g) spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 can (28 oz or 800 g) of crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions, until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta cooking water, then drain the spaghetti and set it aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using), and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the tomato sauce and toss to coat. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta cooking water to thin it out.
  5. Serve the spaghetti hot, garnished with fresh chopped basil leaves (if using).
